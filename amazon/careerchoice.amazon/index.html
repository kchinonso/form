What is Amazon Career Choice Expansion Program?

Originally designed to support Amazon employees, the Career Choice program has entered a new phase—opening its doors to workers from partner companies and independent applicants in select industries.

Amazon believes in the power of upskilling as a universal right. That’s why we’re extending this opportunity beyond our own teams. Whether you're in logistics, healthcare, tech, or business services, you may be eligible for exclusive tuition funding, certification paths, and direct job placement support under the new Workforce Equity Initiative.

What do we cover?

Foundations

English language proficiency or other foundational skills like a high school diploma or GED that helps employees take the next steps towards career success.

Pathways

Specially curated programs designed to help employees learn new skills that open doors to in-demand jobs in fields such as healthcare, transportation, technology, and more.

College

Employees have the opportunity to study at their own pace while earning credits towards an associate’s degree, bachelor’s degree, or academic certificate.

Coaching

Career Choice partners with Kaplan to help employees achieve career goals no matter where they are in their journey through career coaching, college advising, and specialized career services.

We are rooting for you!

Amazon is committed to helping employees access educational and skills training opportunities they can use to grow their careers. We look forward to celebrating their accomplishments.

Interested in joining our school network?

Career Choice partners with educators to ensure employees are set up for success for the next steps in their career. Career Choice has a rigorous selection process with a focus on helping employees through their learning paths, providing job placement assistance, and offering educational programs that lead to their career success.

If your school is interested in partnering with Amazon Career Choice, please complete our School Partner Intake Form. A member of our Career Choice education team will contact you with more information.

Looking to hire skilled employees?

Career Choice collaborates with our employer partners to create ongoing pipelines of skilled candidates. We are looking for multi-year employer partners who have current openings in Healthcare, Tech, Mechanical & Industrial Systems, Administration & Business Services, and Transportation for our Career Choice graduates.
